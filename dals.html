DALS

Dal or dhal, just like posole (or pozole) is both an ingredient and a dish: it refers to a type of dried split pea or lentil and the deeply spiced stew made from simmering the split peas until nicely broken down. What dal you use varies depending on what region of India you visit, pigeon peas (Toor dal) and mung beans (Mung dal) are common in the south, yellow split peas (Chana dal) are used in the north.
